Common Pitfalls and How to Avoid Them

Pitfall 1: Over-Automation

Problem: Trying to automate everything at once

Solution: Start with high-impact automation (policy generation, evidence collection) and expand gradually

Pitfall 2: Poor AI Training

Problem: Not providing enough company-specific data

Solution: Invest time in training AI models with your actual processes and requirements

Pitfall 3: Ignoring Human Oversight

Problem: Trusting AI outputs without review

Solution: Always have human experts review AI-generated policies and assessments

Pitfall 4: Tool Overload

Problem: Using too many AI tools that don't integrate well

Solution: Choose 1-2 comprehensive platforms rather than many specialized tools

Pitfall 5: Neglecting Updates

Problem: Not keeping AI models updated with new regulations

Solution: Regularly update AI training data and review outputs for accuracy
